Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Service Exchange?

Say you really need a haircut, but don't have the budget for it. You are well-versed in carpentry and plumbing, though. A local hairdresser needs their toilet repaired. So, you barter with them to exchange three haircuts for your plumbing services. The Service Exchange offers a place for community members to trade & barter these services.

Do I need to register in advance?

It's not required, but we do encourage it so we know how many people to expect. You can sign up to attend here.

Do I need to pay to attend?

We want to create an environment free from traditional currency. There is no monetary cost for attendance, but we ask that each person come with a service to provide others.

What if I don't have a service to trade?

All are welcome to attend the event, but in order to receive services from others you must have something to offer in exchange. Remember, a service can be anything! From household services like cleaning or organizing to running errands like grocery shopping.
